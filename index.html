Permissions and Their Usage

QuickBars for Home Assistant requires certain sensitive permissions to provide its core functionality. We are committed to using these permissions only for their stated purpose.

Accessibility Service (BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE)

This is the most sensitive permission the app uses.

  • Purpose: The Accessibility Service is used exclusively to detect key presses from your remote control. This allows You to assign specific remote buttons (e.g., the red button) to trigger actions You configure, such as opening a QuickBar or toggling a Home Assistant entity.

  • What We DO NOT Do: The Accessibility Service is NOT used to:

    • Read any content on your screen.
    • See or log any text You type.
    • Monitor your actions or app usage.
    • Collect any personal information.

    The service's scope is strictly limited to capturing hardware key events to provide the trigger functionality.

Draw Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)

  • Purpose: This permission is required to display the QuickBar overlays on top of other applications on your Android TV. This is a core feature of the app, allowing You to access your Home Assistant controls from anywhere.

Internet (INTERNET)

  • Purpose: This permission is used to establish a connection from the app to your own Home Assistant server over your local network or the internet, based on the URL You provide. No data is sent to Us or any other third-party service.
